About TSA Axiom Alloys

TSA Axiom Alloys was founded in 2011 by Gajinder Singh Chohan. We operate from Ludhiana and make metal castings for agricultural equipment and industrial machinery. Our team size is 26–50 employees and the shop floor is proprietor-run for quick decisions and direct follow-up.

Mission

To deliver reliable castings on schedule, with clear quotes and straightforward communication.

Quality & testing

We run routine inspections in our in-house lab, perform dimensional checks, and test material properties as required by the drawing or buyer specification. (If you want specific test names and equipment listed, please supply them and we will add them verbatim.)
